HOW IT WORKS
Two ways to tell the engine which pixels belong to each zone:
🎨 PICK COLOR — Best for body paint (large solid areas)
Click a body color on the paint → assign to zone → engine finds all matching pixels
🖌️ DRAW REGION — Best for numbers, sponsors, artwork
Select a zone → switch to Draw Region or Rectangle → paint over the area on the UV map
All drawn pixels get the same finish regardless of their color
🎯 MAGIC WAND — Smart select for numbers, logos, shapes
Click on a number or logo → auto-selects all connected pixels of similar color
Shift+click to add more areas. Adjust tolerance slider for tighter/wider selection. Ctrl+Z to undo.
WORKFLOW:
1. Load paint image → 2. Body colors: Pick Color mode, click each body color → 3. Numbers/logos: Magic Wand to auto-select, or Draw Region to paint manually → 4. Assign finishes → 5. Generate Script
TIP: Delete zones you don't need (X button). Custom Art slots are for special elements like a logo S that deserves its own holographic finish. PRIORITY: Zone 1 claims pixels first. Later zones get what's left. Drawn regions ALWAYS win over color picks.
